Our Story

History of the College

Y.S.R. Engineering College (YSREC), established in 2008, is a constituent college of Yogi Vemana University, Proddatur. The college was founded with the mission of providing quality engineering education to the rural and semi-urban student population of the Rayalaseema region of Andhra Pradesh.

Named after the former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Dr. Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy, the college embodies his vision of accessible higher education for all. Over the years, YSREC has grown into a respected institution known for its dedicated faculty, rigorous academic programs, and commitment to producing industry-ready engineers.

The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated to Yogi Vemana University. It offers six undergraduate B.Tech programs and one M.Tech program, all designed to meet the evolving needs of the engineering profession.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

visibility

Vision

To emerge as a premier engineering institution that nurtures technically competent, ethically responsible, and socially conscious engineers who contribute to the advancement of society and the nation.

flag

Mission

  • Provide high-quality engineering education through well-designed curricula and modern teaching methodologies.
  • Foster a culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.
  • Produce graduates with strong ethical values, leadership skills, and a commitment to lifelong learning.
  • Serve the community by addressing local and regional technological challenges through collaborative projects.
