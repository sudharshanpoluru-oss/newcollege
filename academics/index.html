Academic Calendar

The academic year follows the semester system as prescribed by Yogi Vemana University. Each academic year consists of two semesters — Odd (July–November) and Even (December–April).

  • Odd Semester: July – November
  • Even Semester: December – April
  • Summer Vacation: May – June
  • Mid-Semester Break: As per university schedule

Examinations

The examination system includes continuous internal assessment and end-semester examinations. Internal assessment contributes 40% and semester-end examinations contribute 60% to the final grade.

  • Internal Exams: Two mid-term exams per semester
  • External Exams: End-semester university examinations
  • Lab Exams: Continuous evaluation + viva voce
  • Project Work: Evaluation by internal & external examiners

Curriculum
