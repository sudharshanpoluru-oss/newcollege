Admissions

Begin your engineering journey at Y.S.R. Engineering College. Learn about our admission process, eligibility, and fee structure.

How to Apply

Admission Process

Admissions to B.Tech programs are conducted through a transparent and merit-based process as per government regulations.

01

Appear for EAPCET

Candidates must qualify in the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET) conducted by APSCHE.

02

Apply for Counselling

Register for AP EAPCET counselling through the official web portal. Fill in your preferences and choose YSREC as your preferred institution.

03

Certificate Verification

Attend the document verification camp along with original certificates. Verify your eligibility for the chosen course and category.

04

Seat Allotment

Based on your rank, preferences, and availability, a seat will be allotted. Accept the allotment and report to the college within the stipulated time.

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

school

Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed or appeared for the Intermediate (10+2) examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as core subjects from a recognized board.

score

Minimum Marks

General category candidates must have secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved categories) in the qualifying examination.

fact_check

Entrance Exam

A valid rank in AP EAPCET is mandatory for admission. Candidates with a Diploma (Lateral Entry) may also apply through ECET counselling.

monitor_heart

Age Limit

There is no upper age limit for admission. Candidates must meet the domicile requirements as specified by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
