Chancellor

The highest constitutional authority of the university, guiding the institution toward academic excellence and societal impact.

Chancellor

Sri S. Abdul Nazeer

His Excellency the Chancellor
Yogi Vemana University, Proddatur - 516360, Andhra Pradesh, India

His Excellency Sri S. Abdul Nazeer is the Chancellor of Yogi Vemana University. The Chancellor is the constitutional head of the university and presides over its convocations. As the highest authority, the Chancellor provides strategic direction and ensures that the university upholds its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and service to society.

Under the Chancellor's esteemed guidance, Y.S.R. Engineering College continues to fulfill its mission of providing accessible, high-quality engineering education to the students of the Rayalaseema region.

From the Chancellor

Chancellor's Message

Education is the most powerful tool for transforming society and empowering individuals. Yogi Vemana University, through its constituent colleges including Y.S.R. Engineering College, remains steadfast in its commitment to providing quality education that nurtures not only intellectual growth but also character, ethics, and social responsibility.

I am delighted to see the progress that Y.S.R. Engineering College has made since its establishment. The college has become a beacon of hope and opportunity for students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds, offering them a platform to realize their dreams and contribute meaningfully to the nation's development.

I extend my best wishes to the faculty, staff, and students of Y.S.R. Engineering College and encourage them to continue striving for excellence in all their endeavors.

Sri S. Abdul Nazeer Chancellor, Yogi Vemana University, Proddatur
