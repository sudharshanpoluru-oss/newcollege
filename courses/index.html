Courses Offered

Y.S.R. Engineering College offers seven undergraduate B.Tech programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills for a successful engineering career.

Our Programs

B.Tech Programs (4 Years)

All programs are approved by AICTE and affiliated to Yogi Vemana University. The curriculum is regularly updated to meet industry standards.

foundation

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering at YSREC covers structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation, environmental engineering, and water resources. Students gain hands-on experience through laboratory work, field visits, and industry projects, preparing them for careers in infrastructure development and construction management.

Computer Science & Engineering

The CSE program provides a strong foundation in programming, data structures, algorithms, database systems, networking, and artificial intelligence. Students work with modern computing environments and are prepared for careers in software development, IT services, research, and higher education.

schedule4 Years groupIntake: 60

Electronics & Communication Engineering

ECE at YSREC focuses on electronic devices, communication systems, signal processing, VLSI design, and embedded systems. The curriculum balances theory with practical lab sessions, enabling students to excel in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and core electronics industries.

schedule4 Years groupIntake: 60

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

EEE offers comprehensive training in power systems, electrical machines, control systems, renewable energy, and power electronics. Graduates are equipped for careers in power generation, transmission, distribution, and the growing renewable energy sector.

schedule4 Years groupIntake: 60

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering program covers thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, manufacturing processes, machine design, and CAD/CAM. Students develop practical skills through workshops and laboratory work, preparing them for roles in manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and energy sectors.

schedule4 Years groupIntake: 60
science

Metallurgy & Material Technology

This specialized program focuses on extraction metallurgy, physical metallurgy, mechanical metallurgy, and materials science. Students study the behavior of metals, alloys, and advanced materials, with applications in manufacturing, aerospace, defense, and research laboratories.

schedule4 Years groupIntake: 30

Science & Humanities

The Science & Humanities department provides foundational courses in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English to all engineering branches. The department also offers elective courses in humanities and management, fostering well-rounded engineers with strong communication and analytical skills.
