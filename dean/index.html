Dean - Faculty of Engineering

Prof. C. Nagaraju

Yogi Vemana University, Proddatur

Prof. C. Nagaraju serves as the Dean of the Faculty of Engineering at Yogi Vemana University. As Dean, he provides academic leadership and coordination for all engineering programs offered by the university and its constituent colleges, including Y.S.R. Engineering College.

The Dean of Faculty of Engineering plays a crucial role in curriculum development, quality assurance, faculty recruitment, and the promotion of research and innovation in engineering education.

From the Dean

Dean's Message

Engineering is not merely a profession — it is a calling to solve problems, create value, and improve the quality of life for society. At the Faculty of Engineering of Yogi Vemana University, we are dedicated to preparing our students to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in engineering fundamentals while encouraging innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on learning. Through our constituent college, Y.S.R. Engineering College, we offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs that are aligned with industry needs and global standards.

I am confident that our students will emerge as competent engineers and responsible citizens who will make meaningful contributions to society. I wish all students the very best in their academic journey.

Prof. C. Nagaraju Dean - Faculty of Engineering, Yogi Vemana University
