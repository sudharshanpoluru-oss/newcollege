About the Department

Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering was established in 2008 to educate and train students in the art and science of civil engineering. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program in Civil Engineering with an annual intake of 60 students.

The curriculum encompasses structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, water resources, and construction management. Students receive comprehensive training in analysis, design, and project management through a blend of theory and practical work.

The department has well-qualified faculty with expertise in diverse areas of civil engineering. Field visits to ongoing construction projects, industrial training, and internships are integral parts of the program, providing students with real-world exposure.
