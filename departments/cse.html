About the Department

Department of CSE

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in 2009 with the vision to produce skilled computing professionals who can contribute to the IT industry and society. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program in Computer Science & Engineering with an annual intake of 60 students.

The curriculum covers a broad range of topics including programming languages, data structures, algorithms, database systems, computer networks, web technologies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity. Students gain hands-on experience through laboratory work, mini-projects, and a major project in their final year.

The department has well-qualified faculty members with expertise in various domains of computer science. Regular workshops, hackathons, and coding competitions are organized to enhance the technical and problem-solving skills of students. The department also maintains strong industry linkages for internships and placements.
