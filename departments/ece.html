About the Department

Department of ECE

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was established in 2008 to address the growing demand for skilled professionals in the electronics and communication sector. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program with an annual intake of 60 students, and a two-year M.Tech program in Digital Electronics & Communication Systems.

The curriculum covers core areas such as analog and digital electronics, signal processing, communication systems, VLSI design, embedded systems, and IoT. Students receive rigorous training through theory and extensive laboratory work that bridges the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements.

The department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories and a team of highly qualified faculty members who are actively engaged in research and consultancy. Students are encouraged to participate in national-level technical competitions, paper presentations, and project exhibitions.
