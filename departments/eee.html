About the Department

Department of EEE

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering was established in 2009 with the mission to produce competent electrical engineers capable of meeting the demands of the power sector and allied industries. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program with an annual intake of 60 students.

The curriculum covers core areas such as electrical machines, power systems, control systems, power electronics, renewable energy systems, and instrumentation. Students undergo extensive laboratory work and project-based learning that reinforces theoretical concepts with practical applications.

The department has a team of qualified faculty members and well-equipped laboratories. Industrial visits, internships, and training programs are regularly organized to enhance the practical knowledge and employability of students.
