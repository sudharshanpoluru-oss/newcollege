About the Department

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 2009 with the objective of producing competent mechanical engineers who can contribute to the manufacturing, automotive, and energy sectors. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program with an annual intake of 60 students.

The curriculum covers core areas such as engineering mechanics, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, manufacturing processes, CAD/CAM, robotics, and renewable energy systems. Students develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills through a balanced mix of theory and laboratory work.

The department has well-equipped workshops and laboratories, and the faculty members bring rich experience from academia and industry. Industrial training, project work, and technical visits form an integral part of the learning experience.
