About the Department

Department of MMT

The Department of Metallurgy & Material Technology was established in 2022 to address the growing need for skilled metallurgists and materials engineers in industries such as aerospace, automotive, defense, and manufacturing. The department offers a four-year B.Tech program with an annual intake of 30 students.

The curriculum covers extraction metallurgy, physical metallurgy, mechanical metallurgy, materials science, welding technology, corrosion engineering, and advanced materials. Students gain hands-on experience through laboratory work, industry visits, and project-based learning.

The department is equipped with specialized laboratories and has qualified faculty members committed to providing quality education and research guidance. The relatively newer program is designed to align with current industry requirements and emerging material technologies.
