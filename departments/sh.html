About the Department

Department of Science & Humanities

The Department of Science & Humanities (S&H) plays a pivotal role in providing the foundational knowledge required for engineering education. The department comprises four streams: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English, each contributing essential theoretical and practical knowledge to engineering students during their first year of study.

The department is responsible for teaching basic science and humanities courses to all first-year B.Tech students, laying a strong foundation in physics, chemistry, mathematics, and communication skills. These courses are designed to develop analytical thinking, scientific temper, and effective communication abilities.

Faculty members are highly qualified and actively engaged in research in their respective fields. The department organizes seminars, workshops, and extension lectures to enhance the learning experience and expose students to the latest developments in science and technology.
