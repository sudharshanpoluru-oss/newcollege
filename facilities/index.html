Facilities & Infrastructure

Our campus is equipped with modern amenities and infrastructure that foster an ideal environment for learning, research, and personal growth.

Campus Amenities

Everything You Need to Succeed

From well-stocked libraries to cutting-edge laboratories, YSREC provides comprehensive facilities that support academic excellence and holistic development.

local_library

Central Library

Our well-stocked central library houses over 35,000 volumes, subscribes to 80+ national and international journals, and provides access to digital resources including IEEE, Springer, and Science Direct databases. The library features separate reading halls for students and faculty, a digital resource centre, and a book bank scheme for economically weaker students.

  • 35,000+ books & reference volumes
  • 80+ print & online journals
  • Digital library with 50+ systems
  • Book bank scheme
  • Reprography facility
computer

Computer Labs

The college maintains six fully-equipped computer laboratories with 250+ high-performance systems configured with the latest software, programming tools, and design suites. All labs have high-speed internet connectivity, LCD projectors, and are networked on a campus-wide LAN.

  • 6 computer labs with 250+ systems
  • Latest configuration with SSD & i7 processors
  • Licensed software & development tools
  • Programming, DBMS, networking & design labs
  • 24x7 UPS backup
sports_tennis

Sports Facilities

YSREC encourages physical fitness and sportsmanship through excellent sports infrastructure. The campus features grounds for cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, and athletics. Indoor sports facilities include table tennis, chess, carroms, and a gymnasium.

  • Cricket & football grounds
  • Basketball & volleyball courts
  • Indoor games room
  • Gymnasium with modern equipment
  • Annual sports meet
hotel

Hostel Accommodation

Separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls within the campus premises. The hostels provide comfortable accommodation with modern amenities including 24x7 water supply, power backup, Wi-Fi connectivity, mess facilities, and recreational areas. Security is ensured with round-the-clock guards and CCTV surveillance.

  • Separate boys & girls hostels
  • 24x7 water & power backup
  • Wi-Fi enabled rooms
  • Hygienic mess with nutritious meals
  • CCTV surveillance & security
restaurant

Canteen & Cafeteria

The college canteen serves hygienic and nutritious food at affordable prices. The spacious cafeteria offers a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals, snacks, and beverages. The canteen operates on a no-profit basis and is managed by a committee comprising faculty and student representatives.

  • Spacious, hygienic dining area
  • Nutritious meals at subsidised rates
  • Variety of cuisines & refreshments
  • Pure drinking water & RO facility
  • Student-managed feedback system
directions_bus

Transport

The college operates a fleet of buses that ply on major routes covering Proddatur town and surrounding villages, ensuring safe and reliable commute for students and staff. The transport facility is especially beneficial for day scholars residing in remote areas.

  • College bus fleet covering major routes
  • Safe & punctual service
  • Special trips for academic events
  • Ample parking for two-wheelers & cars
  • Covered bicycle stand
theater_comedy

Auditorium & Seminar Halls

The college has a spacious multi-purpose auditorium with a seating capacity of 800, equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual systems, stage lighting, and air conditioning. Additionally, two seminar halls with 150-seat capacity each are available for departmental events, workshops, and conferences.

  • 800-seat air-conditioned auditorium
  • Professional audio-visual system
  • Two seminar halls (150 seats each)
  • Video conferencing facility
  • Green room & backstage areas
science

Department Laboratories

Each engineering department has dedicated, well-equipped laboratories designed to provide hands-on practical experience. The labs are equipped with modern instruments, simulation software, test rigs, and measurement equipment that meet AICTE and university curriculum requirements.

  • Dedicated labs for each department
  • Modern test rigs & simulation tools
  • MATLAB, AutoCAD, Multisim & more
  • Material testing & research equipment
  • Regular maintenance & equipment upgrade
wifi

Wi-Fi Campus

The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled with high-speed internet connectivity through a dedicated leased line of 100 Mbps. Students and faculty can access the internet seamlessly from classrooms, library, laboratories, hostels, and open areas for research and academic purposes.

  • 100 Mbps dedicated leased line
  • Wi-Fi access across entire campus
  • Filtered & monitored internet usage
  • 24x7 connectivity in hostels
  • Centralized network management
local_hospital

Medical Facilities

The college provides first-aid and basic medical facilities through a dedicated health centre on campus. A qualified nurse is available during college hours, and arrangements have been made with nearby hospitals for emergency medical care. Regular health check-up camps are organized for students and staff.

  • On-campus health centre
  • Qualified nurse & first-aid
  • Tie-up with nearby hospitals
  • Annual health check-up camps
  • Awareness programs & yoga sessions
