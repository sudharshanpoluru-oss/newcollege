Grievance Redressal

We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly, fairly, and confidentially.

Process

How It Works

Y.S.R. Engineering College has established a Grievance Redressal Committee to address student concerns in a transparent and timely manner. All submissions are reviewed confidentially.

  • 01
    Submit Form

    Fill out the grievance form with accurate details about your concern.

  • 02
    Acknowledgment

    You will receive an acknowledgment within 48 hours of submission.

  • 03
    Review & Resolution

    The committee reviews your grievance and works toward resolution within 15 working days.

  • 04
    Feedback

    You will be notified of the outcome and may appeal if unsatisfied.
