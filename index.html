About the College

Shaping the engineers and leaders of tomorrow since 1999.

Y.S.R. Engineering College (YSREC) is a constituent college of Yogi Vemana University, located in Proddatur, Y.S.R. Kadapa District, Andhra Pradesh. The college was established with a vision to provide quality engineering education to the rural and semi-urban youth of the Rayalaseema region.

Affiliated to JNTUA Anantapur and later merged with Yogi Vemana University, the college offers B.Tech programs in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, and Computer Science Engineering, along with M.Tech and MBA programs.

The campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, a central library, sports facilities, and a vibrant learning environment that fosters both academic and extracurricular excellence.
