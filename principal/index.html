Principal

The academic and administrative head of Y.S.R. Engineering College, leading the institution toward excellence.

Principal

Prof. G. Jaya Chandra Reddy

Professor
Y.S.R Engineering College of Yogi Vemana University
Proddatur - 516360, Andhra Pradesh, India

Prof. G. Jaya Chandra Reddy serves as the Principal of Y.S.R. Engineering College, a constituent college of Yogi Vemana University. As the academic and administrative head of the institution, he provides strategic leadership and direction to all departments and functions of the college.

With a distinguished career in engineering education and administration, Prof. Reddy is committed to fostering an environment of academic rigor, innovation, and holistic student development.

From the Principal

Principal's Message

Welcome to Y.S.R. Engineering College — a place where dreams are nurtured, talents are honed, and future leaders are shaped. Our institution is committed to providing an environment that fosters academic rigor, creative thinking, and holistic development.

We believe that engineering education is not just about technical knowledge but also about building character, ethical values, and a sense of social responsibility. Our dedicated faculty and modern infrastructure ensure that every student receives the guidance and resources needed to excel.

I am proud of the achievements of our students and faculty and am confident that Y.S.R. Engineering College will continue to set benchmarks in engineering education. I invite you to explore our campus, discover our programs, and become a part of the YSREC family. Together, let us build a brighter future for ourselves, our community, and our nation.

Prof. G. Jaya Chandra Reddy Principal, Y.S.R. Engineering College
