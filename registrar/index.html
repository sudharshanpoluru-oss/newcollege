Registrar

The custodian of academic records and administrative operations of the university.

Registrar (i/c)

Prof. P. Padma

Yogi Vemana University, Kadapa

Prof. P. Padma serves as the Registrar (In-charge) of Yogi Vemana University. As the custodian of the university's academic records and administrative functions, the Registrar plays a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of all statutory and administrative processes of the university and its constituent colleges.

With a wealth of experience in academic administration, Prof. Padma oversees examinations, student records, certifications, and the implementation of university policies and regulations.

From the Registrar

Registrar's Message

It is my responsibility and privilege to serve as the Registrar of Yogi Vemana University. Our office is committed to maintaining the highest standards of accuracy, transparency, and efficiency in all administrative and academic record-keeping functions.

At Y.S.R. Engineering College, we strive to ensure that all administrative processes — from admissions to examinations to certification — are conducted smoothly and in accordance with the established rules and regulations of the university.

I assure all students, faculty, and stakeholders of our continued dedication to providing prompt and reliable administrative services. We welcome your feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Prof. P. Padma Registrar (i/c), Yogi Vemana University
