Vice-Chancellor

Prof. Raja Shekhar Bellamkonda

Ph.D, Professor
Yogi Vemana University, Kadapa - 516005, Andhra Pradesh, India

Prof. Raja Shekhar Bellamkonda serves as the Vice-Chancellor of Yogi Vemana University. As the chief executive officer of the university, he is responsible for providing visionary leadership and strategic direction to all academic and administrative activities of the university and its constituent colleges, including Y.S.R. Engineering College.

Under his stewardship, the university continues to strengthen its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and community engagement.

From the Vice-Chancellor

Vice-Chancellor's Message

It is my privilege to lead Yogi Vemana University, an institution dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and societal transformation. Our constituent college, Y.S.R. Engineering College, stands as a testament to our commitment to providing quality engineering education to students from all walks of life.

In today's rapidly evolving world, engineering education must go beyond technical proficiency. We must nurture critical thinking, ethical reasoning, and a spirit of innovation in our students. At Y.S.R. Engineering College, we strive to create an environment where students are encouraged to question, explore, and create.

I am proud of the achievements of our faculty and students and am confident that Y.S.R. Engineering College will continue to produce engineers who will contribute significantly to the nation's progress. I extend my warm wishes to all members of the YSREC community.

Prof. Raja Shekhar Bellamkonda Vice-Chancellor, Yogi Vemana University
