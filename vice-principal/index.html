Vice-Principal

Supporting the academic and administrative leadership of Y.S.R. Engineering College.

Vice-Principal

Prof. K Venkata Ramanaiah

Y.S.R. Engineering College, Proddatur

Prof. K Venkata Ramanaiah serves as the Vice-Principal of Y.S.R. Engineering College. In this role, he assists the Principal in the academic and administrative management of the college, with a focus on maintaining high standards of teaching, learning, and student development.

His responsibilities include overseeing academic affairs, coordinating faculty development programs, managing student activities, and ensuring the smooth implementation of curriculum and examination schedules across all departments.

From the Vice-Principal

Vice-Principal's Message

At Y.S.R. Engineering College, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers who will drive innovation and progress. Our focus extends beyond academic excellence to include character building, ethical values, and holistic development of every student.

I believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness, and it is our responsibility as educators to provide the right environment, guidance, and opportunities for them to realize their full potential. Our faculty members are dedicated mentors who go beyond the call of duty to support students in their academic and personal growth.

I encourage all students to make the most of their time at YSREC, to participate in co-curricular and extracurricular activities, and to develop the skills and confidence needed to excel in their chosen careers.

Prof. K Venkata Ramanaiah Vice-Principal, Y.S.R. Engineering College
